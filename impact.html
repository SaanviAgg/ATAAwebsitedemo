FORGE 4421 – Impact

Making a Difference Through Robotics

Community Outreach

FORGE 4421 is deeply rooted in community involvement. As a city-wide community robotics team, we unite students from various schools to promote STEM learning and collaboration. Through workshops, demonstrations, and partnerships, we share our passion for engineering and innovation with younger students and local organizations.

STEM Education & Mentorship

Our team dedicates significant time to mentoring younger teams in the FLL and FTC divisions, helping them develop technical skills and leadership abilities. We also organize events that introduce coding, design, and problem-solving to students across Alberta.

Equity & Inclusion

FORGE 4421 is committed to ensuring all youth—regardless of gender, background, or school—have equal access to robotics and STEM opportunities. We’re proud to foster a diverse environment where creativity, teamwork, and innovation thrive together.

Global Inspiration

Competing internationally allows us to represent Alberta’s innovation on a global stage. We strive not only to build competitive robots but also to build connections, share ideas, and inspire others to pursue careers in science and technology.
