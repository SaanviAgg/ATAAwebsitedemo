We are Team 4334, Alberta Tech Alliance (ATA), an FRC robotics team based in Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

We strive to actively improve ourselves and others by spreading FIRST Robotics ideals and values, both in and out of competition.

ATA started with one student — Alex Rodrigues — and in 2012, they made history as a rookie team reaching Einstein at the World Championship.

Historical Highlights

  • 2012: World Championship Finalists with 1114 and 2056 – the legendary "Eh Team"
  • 2013–2016: Multiple regional wins, technical awards, and IRI Champion
  • 2017–2019: Consistent World Championship qualifiers, engineering awards
  • 2020: Season shortened by COVID, but took home Judges' Award
